Before your first visit

We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes early to complete your new-patient forms. You can also download and fill them in advance — see the link below.

What to bring

  • Your insurance card and a valid photo ID
  • A list of any medications you're currently taking
  • Any dental X-rays from a previous provider (if available)
  • Completed new-patient forms (or arrive 15 min early)

Insurance we accept

We are in-network with most major PPO plans and verify your benefits before your visit so there are no surprises. We also see patients without insurance — ask us about our in-office savings options.

Accepted plans include

  • Delta Dental PPO & Premier
  • Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian
  • BlueCross BlueShield
  • Most other major PPO plans
  • CareCredit and flexible payment options

What to expect on your first visit

Your first appointment is all about getting to know you and your smile. We'll take a full set of digital X-rays (if it's been a while), do a thorough exam, and talk through any areas of concern — without pressure to begin treatment on day one.

Most first visits take about 60–90 minutes. You'll leave with a clear picture of your dental health and a plan that fits your goals and budget.
